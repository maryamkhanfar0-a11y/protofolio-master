Notable Projects

  1. The Shadow of the Nakba

    Graduation Project

    A documentary exploring the repeated displacement of people from refugee camps in the northern West Bank. It tells the personal stories of individuals from Jenin, Tulkarem, and Nur Shams camps. The film highlights their daily struggles and resilience, drawing a connection between the 1948 Nakba and the current wave of displacement. Archival footage, symbolic visuals, and strong imagery are used to emphasize the human side of the story and preserve collective memory.
